Overview

We release a total of $57$ single-task expert datasets with transition data from the replay buffers of single-task SimbaV2 agents. The domains consist of MuJoCo (5), DMControl (28), MyoSuite (10), and HumanoidBench (14), encompassing a variety of locomotion and manipulation tasks with varying levels of complexity. The dataset details are summarized below.

Domains Tasks Embodiments Episodes Transitions Size Download
